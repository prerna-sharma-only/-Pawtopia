# Pawtopia    
### The Dog Hub

> <img src="https://readme-typing-svg.demolab.com?font=Georgia&size=20&duration=3500&pause=1200&color=8B5E3C&vCenter=true&width=520&lines=Where+every+breed+finds+its+spotlight." alt="Typing Animation"/>

A modern dog exploration platform built with **Node.js**, **Express.js**, **EJS**, and the **Dog CEO API**. Designed to help users discover breeds, explore beautiful dog imagery, and enjoy a smooth browsing experience through a clean, responsive interface.

<h2><img src="https://img.icons8.com/ios-filled/20/8B5E3C/dog-paw-print.png" alt="paw" /> Pawprint</h2>
<p><sub>The heart of Pawtopia.</sub></p>

Pawtopia transforms live data from the **Dog CEO API** into an engaging dog discovery experience. From browsing every breed to exploring random images and sub-breeds, every page is rendered dynamically with Express and EJS while maintaining a warm, elegant design inspired by the bond between humans and dogs.
